Lasagna

Description

Indulge your taste buds with a mouthwatering lasagna recipe that will delight both family and friends. Begin by layering thin sheets of al dente pasta, generously smothered with a rich and savory Bolognese sauce. Each layer is then lovingly adorned with a creamy ricotta and mozzarella cheese blend, creating a heavenly combination of flavors. For added depth, sprinkle a hint of aromatic herbs and spices, such as oregano and basil, infusing the dish with a tantalizing aroma. Finally, bake to perfection until the cheese melts into a golden, bubbling masterpiece. With its comforting warmth and harmonious blend of textures, this lasagna recipe promises to satisfy every craving and create lasting memories around the dinner table.

Ingredients

  • 1 small chicken breast, cooked and shredded
  • 1/4 cup diced carrots
  • 1/4 cup frozen peas
  • 1/4 cup diced potatoes
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 sheet of pre-made puff pastry

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven and grease ramekin or dish.
  2. Melt butter, cook onions until translucent.
  3. Add carrots, peas, and potatoes. Stir in flour.
  4. Pour in chicken broth and milk, add thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Cook until thickened.
  5. Remove from heat, add shredded chicken. Stir well.
  6. Transfer mixture to the dish.
  7. Roll out puff pastry, place on top, seal edges.
  8. Make slits on the pastry for steam.
  9. Bake until golden and bubbly.
  10. Cool briefly, then serve and enjoy!
