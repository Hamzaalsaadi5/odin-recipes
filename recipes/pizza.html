Pizza

Description

Discover the irresistible flavors of Neapolitan pizza with this authentic recipe that will transport you to the bustling streets of Naples. Begin with a classic thin and chewy pizza dough made from flour, water, yeast, and salt, allowing it to rise to perfection. Once ready, generously spread a vibrant tomato sauce made from ripe San Marzano tomatoes, delicately seasoned with garlic and basil. Top it off with fresh mozzarella cheese, drizzled with a glimmer of extra virgin olive oil, and sprinkled with aromatic oregano. Finally, bake the pizza in a blazing-hot oven until the crust achieves that coveted charred and blistered appearance. The result is a Neapolitan pizza boasting a harmonious medley of flavors—a crispy yet tender crust, tangy tomato sauce, and creamy melted cheese—leaving you craving more with each delicious bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • San Marzano tomatoes (crushed or pureed)
  • Garlic cloves (minced)
  • Fresh basil leaves (chopped)
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese (sliced or torn)
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to highest temperature with a pizza stone or baking sheet inside.
  2. Shape and stretch pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Combine San Marzano tomatoes, garlic, salt, and basil to make sauce.
  4. Transfer dough to hot stone or baking sheet.
  5. Spread sauce on dough, leaving a small border.
  6. Add mozzarella cheese on top.
  7. Drizzle olive oil and sprinkle dried oregano.
  8. Bake for 10-15 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  9. Let cool briefly, then slice and serve hot.
